# Kair0 – Market Intelligence

Kair0 is an AI-powered platform that reads global news in real time and delivers structured market bias for forex pairs directly to your X (Twitter) feed and Telegram. No noise. Just direction.

## Features

- **24/7 News Monitoring:** Instantly tracks global financial news, including CPI, NFP, rate decisions, and geopolitical events.
- **AI Market Analysis:** Analyzes news events, cross-references affected forex pairs, and generates structured bias, direction, reasoning, key levels, and timeframe.
- **Instant Signal Delivery:** Sends clean, actionable bias reports to your Telegram or X feed in under a minute.
- **Waitlist Signup:** Join the waitlist to get early access and updates.

## How It Works

1. **News drops:** Kair0 monitors global financial news feeds 24/7. When a trigger event occurs, the engine activates.
2. **AI reads the market:** The AI analyzes the event, determines affected pairs, and generates a structured report.
3. **You get the signal:** A bias report is delivered to your Telegram or X feed before the crowd reacts.
